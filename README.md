# Territorial Inquiry

From a question to a map, from a map to an insight.
Exploring places, patterns, and the stories hidden in data.

## Mission

Investigate territorial questions through data, GIS, programming, and spatial analysis, transforming questions into evidence, maps, and insights.

## What is this repository?

Part laboratory, part notebook, part portfolio. The goal is to better understand places through spatial evidence and reproducible analysis.
Territorial Inquiry is a collection of studies, experiments, and reproducible workflows exploring territorial questions through data, GIS, programming, and spatial analysis.

## Principles

- Curiosity driven by questions
- Transparency in methods
- Reproducible workflows
- Evidence-based analysis
- Open knowledge
- Continuous learning
